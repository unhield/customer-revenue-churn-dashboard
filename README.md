## 🔍 Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard focused on analyzing customer revenue behavior and churn patterns.  
The objective is to identify revenue distribution, customer retention risks, and key drivers impacting long-term business value.

## 📌 Key Insights
- A significant portion of revenue is generated from a limited group of high-value customers  
- Customer churn contributes directly to revenue leakage, highlighting retention as a critical growth lever  
- Certain customer segments exhibit higher churn risk, indicating potential targeting opportunities  
- Revenue distribution is uneven, suggesting dependency on a small customer base  
- Retained customers demonstrate consistent revenue contribution over time

Download the `.pbix` file and open it in Power BI Desktop to explore customer behavior, churn patterns, and revenue insights interactively.
